# Brand Spec: Holistic Gold Shop

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Navy Blue)**: `#0F172A` / `oklch(18% 0.03 250)`
- **Accent/CTAs (Premium Gold)**: `#D4AF37` / `oklch(74% 0.14 85)`
- **Background (Off-White)**: `#F8FAFC` / `oklch(98% 0.01 240)`
- **Success (Green)**: `#10B981` / `oklch(70% 0.17 150)`
- **Red/Alert (Red)**: `#EF4444` / `oklch(62% 0.22 25)`

## Typography
- **Clean sans-serif**: Inter (Primary), system-ui
- **Monospaced numbers**: Roboto Mono (tabular-nums)

## Posture & Layout: "The Vault" (Crisp & Heavy)
- **Aesthetic**: High-security banking software, vault-like stability.
- **Borders**: Thick, high-contrast borders (2px) using Navy Blue (#0F172A).
- **Radii**: Sharp to minimal (4px - 6px); avoid overly rounded corners.
- **Density**: High information density; maximize data visibility without clutter.
- **Typography**: Strictly use monospaced fonts (Roboto Mono) for all numeric weights, prices, and IDs.
- **Contrast**: High contrast between Navy, Gold, and Off-White; use Gold sparingly for high-impact actions only.
- **Components**: Heavy-duty buttons, rigid grid systems, and clear demarcation between functional zones.
